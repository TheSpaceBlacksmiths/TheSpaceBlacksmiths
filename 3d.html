The 3D Model

This deviece will be placed on the astronaut's forearm and will be use to control the space suit.

The astronauts will be able to modify and control the suit from the big screen which is also a touch screen that can be use with gloves. On this screen will be also desplayed the gps map system, sort of like google maps that can help the astronauts on their explorations.

The small screen will show the vitals of the astronaut, like heart rate, blood preasure, oxygen level and more...

The buttons will control the modes of each screen, and the big red one will start and shutdown the deviece
